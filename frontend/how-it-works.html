FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Is SkillSphere really free? ▾
Yes, completely free. We don't charge for listings, exchanges, or any features. Our mission is to make learning accessible to everyone.
What if I'm a complete beginner? ▾
Beginners are very welcome! Many people on SkillSphere specifically look for beginners to teach. Just be honest about your level in your listing.
How many sessions does an exchange involve? ▾
That's entirely up to you and your exchange partner. Some do a single 1-hour session, others do ongoing weekly exchanges for months. You negotiate and agree before starting.
What if the exchange doesn't work out? ▾
Either party can end the exchange at any time — no obligations. You can then leave an honest review and find a better match through the marketplace.
Can I list more than one skill? ▾
Absolutely. You can list as many skills as you want. Many members teach 2-3 skills and find exchange partners for each one independently.
Is it safe to meet in person? ▾
We recommend starting with online sessions first to build trust. If you meet in person, always choose a public place for the first session. Use your best judgment.
